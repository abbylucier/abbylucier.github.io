Time

2022

Overview

In Time, I aimed to explore the ways in which the pandemic has affected the feeling of time in my life.The past several years have felt painfully slow yet at the same time unbelievably fast in many ways, leaving me with a complicated relationship to this era of my life. Through these clocks, I attempted to visualize these feelings. While each one references a specific feeling for me, I left the interpretation of each clock up the the viewer, choosing not to label them in any way.

Materials

  • Wood
  • Paint
  • Laser cut chip board
  • Glue

Process

I began using the bandsaw to create the base out of wood. I later moved on to using the laser cutter in order to get precise - yet warped - numbers, which I created using Illustrator. Next, I painted each clock base, as well as the chipboard numbers, and then assembled each clock. I choose to hand them in such a manner in an attempt to invoke time zone clocks which you might see at an Airport - another place where time seems to move in a different way.
